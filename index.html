Creating Partnership One Hoof at a Time

At Peace and Praise Horse Training, we seek to develop the natural partnership of a horse and rider. We believe that God created horses to mirror or relationship with Him. It's a relationship build on trust, love, and submission that results in a breathtaking dance.

Our Training Method Horses in Training

Joining the Dance

Peace and Praise Horse Training not only trains horses, but also trains their riders. A horse is only as good as its rider, so we love to offer lessons to the rider in order to help them learn their horse's language and take part in the dance. 

Former Training Horse "Luna"

Send your horse for training

We offer full board training for $800 a month which includes 25 sessions. We also offer single session training if you would like to bring your horse to us for $30 a session. 

Take a lesson

You can bring your horse to us at take a lesson or you can ride on of our beautiful lesson horses. Lessons are $25 for 60 minute private lessons and $20 for 60 minute group lessons. 

Come for a visit!

Come visit our farm! We love having people come to see the horses, and they love it too. Bring a carrot or two if you want to make a new best friend!

We are located in the stunning mountains of Colorado near Estes National Park.
