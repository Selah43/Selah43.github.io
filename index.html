My Horses

Selah and Shiloh

Selah Dun Gebo- Selah is a 12 year old quarter horse mare. She is 14.2 hands and her color is red dun. Selah is the type of horse that anyone can hop on and have a great time!

Shiloh is a 4 year old quarter horse gelding. He is approaching 16 hands and he is a bay. Shiloh loves to just hang out and cuddle or go for a gallop in the woods!

Both of my horses are beautiful creations of our Almighty God.
